Website 1 The Work Cycle
Brand Minimal - The media and information is presented simply. The navigation tools are clear and typography is not very flashy.
Colorful - To balance out the minimal layout of the site, there is some color to provide the eye something interesting.
Trendy - The combination of both minimal and colorful is very trendy. It gives the site a hip vibe that aligns with the content of the website.
Functionality As the website serves as a gallery of work cyclists, the "Headline and Gallery" format effectively presents the desired media. The grid below the headline displays the photos of workers, cyclists and bicycles.
Interactivity The website serves to display the photos of bicycles in work environments and therefore, the photos are organized in albums. The site provides access to all albums on the homepage. Each album is presented as a row in a grid with some photos already shown and the user must click left and right to browse more photos. Each row consists of an album and as the user scrolls down, more albums keep appearing to give the idea of an endless collection of photos. The minimal effort it takes to browse photos (users do not have to click any other pages to do what they have come to the site to do) indicates that it is a very functional website.
Navigation The navigation is very clear on the site. There are four buttons in the upper right corner directing the user to whatever information they need. However, since the main content of the site are photos of work cyclists, the album navigation provided and "endless page" scrolling allows the user to remain on the homepage for most of their needs. Therefore, the navigation is simple and very effective.

Website 2 Christian Woo
Brand Clean - The layout is very clean with very minimal clutter on the site. The site is in a Full Screen Photo layout and therefore has a big photo in the background and a simple navigation bar at the bottom.
Sleek - This is an architect's portfolio website. His aesthetic seems to be very sleek, modern and sophisticated. Therefore, since he is using a full screen photo, the aesthetic of his architecture carries over into the feel of the site.
Modern - The simplicity of both the architecture and navigation bar help the site feel very modern.
Functionality As an architecture portfolio, the homepage does a good job of immediately showcasing a piece of work to the user. The navigation is the only other element on the site and therefore, the site functions well in directing the user to more information. However, upon entering Collections, a quote pops up as an intro and the button to continue to look at the collection is very small that some may miss it - which may prevent the user from seeing the main point of the site. However, past that, the site showcases the architect's works effectively.
Interactivity There is interaction on the homepage. The full screen photo can be changed by two arrows on the side of the website. Clicking on certain menu options expand the navigation bar upwards to present the relevant information.
Navigation The only real element on the home page aside from the photo is the navigation bar. Therefore, the site is easy to navigate. There are only two navigation options that actually take you away from the homepage but the navigation bar remains on the other pages. Mostly, the user will stay on the homepage.

Website 3 Vladimir Strajnic
Brand Raw - The site is raw in that all the images and bars have an unfinished feel. The images are simple sketches, however this allows the eye to fall on the artist's work which is all presented in color.
Simple - The site focuses on simplicity. There are few lines, few icons and a simple color scheme.
Monotone - The simple color scheme ends up looking a bit monotonous and in turn, bland as well.
Functionality Since this is an artist's portfolio, the main purpose it serves is to showcase the artist's work. Therefore, the site is highly functional. The homepage has a moving image that allows the user to see works the artist wants to showcase along with a description without navigating away from the homepage. Clicking through the site, the information desired is clearly there. The portfolio presents his artwork in a straightforward manner and the bio and contact tabs also provide all information right away.
Interactivity On the homepage, there are buttons to scroll through the showcased works. Also, the hover-over images bring color to the website. Hovering over the navigation bar activates a dropdown menu.
Navigation The navigation is quite simple. There are four options in the top right corner (home, portfolio, biography, contact) and every page has a home button to return to the homepage. On the homepage, there are two options (print and interactive) to direct the user to the type of work he/she would want to view.

Website 4 PANDR
Brand Fun - The website's main image changes between three images and all contain a humorous or illustrator icon type graphic. Combined with the green and black color scheme, the website seems less serious and more fun. The PANDR icon's typography also seems as such.
Bold - The graphics all have clean lines. The green and black color scheme of the main image area against a white background gives the site a sense of boldness.

The simplicity combined with the boldness makes the site seem bright. It is also bright on the other pages below the fold where there is no feature graphic - the site is mainly white with simple typography.
Functionality The main purpose of the site is to present the work of a web design and development company. Each page remains in the featured graphic layout (which I believe should be used only for the homepage) forcing users to scroll down to see information they would want (ie. services offered or previous work). The information below the fold is clearly organized in an infographic like style. The choice of a big font, however, makes the pages way too long.
Interactivity On the main page, there are buttons to scroll through different main images in the carousel. There are standard hover-overs in the navigation bar.
Navigation There are icons at the bottom of the main image carousel that lead to information perhaps most desired by users. There is also a navigation bar at the top right corner that lead to all the information users should need. The logo serves as the home button which may confuse some people.

Website 5 Voicebox Creative
Brand Sophisticated - The black and white grid of the company's previous work is elegantly animated to appear onto the site. The choice of a monotonous color scheme brings about an air of sophistication to the site. The simplicity of the left sidebar navigation also adds to the sophistication.
Sleek - The grid and the black and white color scheme help the site look very sleek. Also, the choice of content to display also helps as the various bottles presented have a shine that adds to the sleekness.
Simple - The content is simple. It is a creative design firm and the information people want to view is simply laid out in a grid on the homepage.
Functionality As stated before, as a design firm, users most likely want to see their previous work. The site displays this information right on the homepage in a very simple grid with more information coming up as you hover over the image.
Interactivity The site is very interactive, all functions on the site are animated. Hovering over an image on the homepage activates a carousel of images in color with a small description at the bottom. On each past project page, there is a project dock at the bottom that pulls up to see more information. Arrows on each side of a past project page engages the user to easily browse through more projects.
Navigation The left sidebar serves as the main navigation for the site. The logo serves as the homepage button. When an option is clicked in the sidebar, a dropdown of more options appears for easy navigation (ie. a list of all the past projects). There are arrows on any past project page so that the user can browse through past projects with ease.

Website 6 Passeig de Gracia
Brand Modern - There are a lot of clean lines that are present on the homepage. The icons and site images all resemble hexagons giving the site a graphic aesthetic.
Bold - The site uses a lot of bold colors and lines that further add to the modern, graphic aesthetic.
Abstract - The use of many shapes and lines add to the artistic and abstract feeling of the page.
Functionality The website is highly functional. The site's main purpose is to promote the artistic community in the city Paseo de Gracia. Therefore, the homepage's main navigation leads users directly to the information they need. When clicking on the events page, a calendar full of events shows up on the right side. On all other pages, icons that are clearly labeled direct users to more content on the site.
Interactivity The site is very interactive on the homepage. There is an image carousel that changes on a timer. However, there are arrows on both sides of the main image that allows the user to browse the preset images from left to right. Also, the icons presented right below the main header image display more information about the icon during a hover over. Other features on the site, like the magazine, are interactive as well - the site allows the user to browse their magazines on their site through a reader, which is very engaging.
Navigation The navigation is very straightforward. There is a navigation banner at the top of the home page and icons all over the site to direct users to information they desire. The header remains throughout the whole site so navigating the site is fairly easy. The icons also display very clearly where they will be taken during a hover over.
